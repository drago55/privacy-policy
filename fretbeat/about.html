About FretBeat

Version: 1.0.0

FretBeat is a versatile metronome and guitar tuner designed for musicians of all levels. With precise BPM control, customizable sounds, and a simple tuner interface, FretBeat helps you stay in rhythm and keep your guitar perfectly tuned.
